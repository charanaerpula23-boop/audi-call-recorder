**Secure Call Recorder** is a  Android application designed to give you full control over your call recordings. Built with modern Android development practices, it ensures that your sensitive conversations never leave your device.

## ✨ Features

*   **Privacy-First & Offline-Only:** Absolutely no cloud syncing, telemetry, or external servers. Your recordings are stored securely and locally on your device hardware.
*   **Smart Recording Rules:** Customize your recording behavior with granular settings:
    *   📞 Record All Numbers
    *   ❓ Record Unknown Numbers Only
    *   👤 Record Selected Contacts Only
*   **Native Playback Experience:** High-quality, built-in audio player to review your recordings instantly without needing external media apps.
*   **Modern Material UI:** A clean, edge-to-edge Material Design 3 interface crafted seamlessly with Jetpack Compose.

## 🏗️ Technical Architecture

*   **Framework:** Android SDK, Kotlin
*   **UI Toolkit:** Jetpack Compose (Material Design 3)
*   **Local Storage:** Room Database (SQLite) for record management, DataStore for user preferences
*   **Media APIs:** Native Android MediaRecorder and MediaPlayer
